title: "3.6.2"

June 26, 2026

---

#### **Agentic Analysis**

Our smart assistant continues to get smarter. Iris is now more deeply integrated with our dashboarding framework, adding capabilities for configuring data-visualizations and providing more visual responses:

- **Widget Support** - A new entry point in the widget editor lets you chat with Iris directly for configuration, with context from the widget you're working on.
- **Dashboarding Sync** - Dashboards and Iris skills now stay in sync automatically, no manual updates needed.

---

#### Overwatch

We are continuing to put effort into our live spatial view, to better and more quickly answer the question "what's where when?":

- **View Tracked Objects** - Live-monitoring is now more informative and detailed, with expanded capability for plotting tracks from integrated GPS devices and real-world locations of visual detections.
- **Track Playback** - Object tracks are controlled from the shared histogram and date-selector, rewind and replay tracks to review movement over time, not just live position.

---

#### **System Management**

We know from experience it takes iteration to get to ideal models, workflows, and insights – focus on the good data without mixing in the bad with new and simpler features to remove cameras, models, and logged activities through both our UI and API:

- **Tidy up Data** - We made it simpler to delete and remove cameras and models from the UI, and added API functionality to clean up agents and sites with additional UI support to follow.
- **Customize your Worlds** - For our partners and other environment super-administrators, we’re giving you the ability to try new features and customize your environment with a new Feature Flags page in the settings menu.

---

#### Platform Performance

We addressed uncommon cases that could lead to temporary service disruptions:

- **Transcoder Recovery** - Edge transcoders now auto-recover after brief network interruptions instead of requiring manual intervention.
- **Startup Stability** - Fixed an issue affecting availability in new environments when a dependent workflow service was unreachable**.**
- **Authorization & Usability** - Addressed rare but widespread UI issues that could lead to stale data displaying until browser refresh and/or users being unexpectedly logged out.
