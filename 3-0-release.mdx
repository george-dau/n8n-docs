March 30, 2026

## Major Release

The next evolution of Worlds delivers on the full AI value-chain, from data aggregation through model training and deployment to actionable insights and embedded solutions. Worlds is your system of record for the real-world, and now provides the full end-to-end system that can continuously iterate and improve – so that your operations can too.

#### New Features

- **Search, Analyze, Discover** — Describe what you’re looking for and find it in video instantly, uncovering insights about activity, assets, and operations. Investigate rumors or validate operational anecdotes with objective data - leverage your findings in models to train, data to capture and report, alerts to action in realtime, or processes to automate.
- **Implement Solutions** — Put Worlds insights to work with intelligent agents, visual workflows, and continuous monitoring. Any non-technical user can now develop solutions for themselves as if they had a team of engineers. Define the activities and data to capture for analytical views, set up realtime alerting to the channels your teams use, or automate actions and processes in external systems, all from within the Worlds platform.
- **Monitor Live** — Never watch a video bay, comb through historical footage, or walk a facility again. With Worlds Overwatch, see your full environment in realtime with a stream of identified events and activities as they occur. Investigate issues live not after the fact, and act in the moment when it counts.
- **Access Insights** — Track what matters most with customizable data visualizations and dashboards. Discover time and location trends in real-world activity, over days, weeks, or months. Review historical insights to inform your next real-world process improvement, then come back to Worlds to measure the impact, capture additional data and insights, and discover the next process improvement to make.

#### **Built on a Solid Foundation**

Prior to the 3.0 release, Worlds platform capabilities led the market in custom vision and operational solutions - turning video and sensor streams into a realtime spatialized intelligence layer, to see what actually happens in real-world operations like never before possible. New features in this release build on and add value to our core competencies:

- **Build your World** — Access intuitive interfaces for spatial configuration, adding sites, importing floor-plans, and defining areas of interest. Create zones in mapped space or within a single camera view, to target in workflows for alerting, spatial insights, and knowing definitively what is where and when. Place and calibrate cameras in space, so that what they see can be accurately mapped to the real world.
- **See What Happens** — Leverage foundation vision models for rapid deployment, or segmentation and tagging interfaces for domain-specific detection capabilities and rapid iteration. Proprietary pre-processing improves performance, lowers cost, and delivers practical solutions with positive return on investment. Proprietary post-processing enriches data for additional value and insight. Never leak confidential data to risky AI models, Worlds models are trained on customers' isolated data alone.
