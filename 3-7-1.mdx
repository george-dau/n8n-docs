title: "3.7.1"
---

July 10, 2026

---

#### **Agentic Analysis**

Another enhancement to our Iris assistant, who is increasingly eager to learn:

- **Suggested Prompts** - Iris now learns business context through Worlds data and user-interaction, replacing generic prompt examples with recommendations tailored to your organization.

---

#### Overwatch

Tracking real-world objects through space and time is smoother, clearer, and simpler:

- **Live Tracking** - Object tracks are now hidden by default and enabled through the filtering menu with loading animations, for a snappier experience when tracking a high-volume of objects.
- **Improved Time Control** - Tracked object positions now animate into place instead of jumping, for a more natural live view and smoother scrubbing through the historical timeline.

---

#### **System Management**

We took steps to make it easier to review cameras and statuses at a glance, as well as remove more data in bulk when no longer needed:

- **Improved Camera View** - The Cameras page in navigation has been enhanced with additional filter options and thumbnails - making it easier to navigate, understand, and troubleshoot datasources.
- **Site Teardown** - Added support for deleting sites in the UI with automatic cleanup of all related data.

---

#### Event Review

We made some minor improvements and fixes to our Event Activity detail view:

- **Video Playback** - Fixed Chronicle assets appearing as static images instead of playable video.

---

#### Platform Performance

We addressed uncommon cases that could lead to temporary service disruptions:

- **Transcoder Recovery** - Edge transcoders now auto-recover after brief network interruptions instead of requiring manual intervention.
- **Startup Stability** - Fixed an issue affecting availability in new environments when a dependent workflow service was unreachable**.**
- **Authorization & Usability** - Addressed rare but widespread UI issues that could lead to stale data displaying until browser refresh and/or users being unexpectedly logged out.
