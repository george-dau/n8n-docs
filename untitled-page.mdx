June 26, 2026

---

#### **Agentic Analysis**

Our smart assistant continues to get smarter. Iris is now more deeply integrated with our dashboarding framework, adding capabilities for configuring data-visualizations and providing more visual responses:

- **Widget Support** - A new entry point in the widget editor lets you chat with Iris directly for configuration, with context from the widget you're working on.
- **Dashboarding Sync** - Dashboards and Iris skills now stay in sync automatically, no manual updates needed.

---

#### Overwatch

The Overwatch view to improve our live-monitoring capabilities:

- **View Tracked Objects** - Live-monitoring view is now more informative and detailed, with expanded capability for plotting tracks from integrated GPS devices and real-world locations of visual detections.

---

#### **System Management**

We continued the rollout of our new Chronicles system for enhanced time-based data capture:

- **Event Activity** - Replacing the legacy events list, the new activity feed includes advanced and persistent filtering now supporting all dimensions of captured events and activity, for faster and more focused discovery.

---

#### Platform Performance

We tackled several stability and accuracy improvements in our core tracking, detection, and training pipelines:

- **Track Stability** - Major rework to our tracker improves unstable detection boxes, fragmented tracks, and track switching issues in complex & high-traffic environments.
- **Job Progress** - Improved system visibility with updated process tracking to accurately reflect true job progress states.
- **Imported Models** - Resolved an issue with class-ordering affecting imported models, improving the scalability and portability of Worlds vision models.
