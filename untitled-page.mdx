June 26, 2026

---

#### **Agentic Analysis / Iris Assistant**

We continued expanding our agentic consultant (for now, lovingly dubbed "Iris") with new UX improvements, skills, and organization-level customization:

- **Focused Interaction** - Introduced an improved text-based conversational interface for AI-powered analysis.
- **Sample Prompt Suggestions** - Iris now surfaces organization-specific prompt suggestions to help teams get started faster.
- **Anomaly Alerting** - New chat-based capability to configure and dispatch email alerts when anomalous situations occur, powered by autonomous monitoring (_Alpha testing_).

---

#### **Dashboards**

We shipped a major new feature to enable boundless data-visualization:

- **Custom Widget Framework** - New built-in code editor allows users to upload and preview code to craft custom visualizations for any specific need, in a framework that provides flexibility without risking performance.

---

#### **Events & Activity**

We continued the rollout of our new Chronicles system for enhanced time-based data capture:

- **Event Activity** - Replacing the legacy events list, the new activity feed includes advanced and persistent filtering now supporting all dimensions of captured events and activity, for faster and more focused discovery.

---

#### Overwatch

We are shifting focus back to the Overwatch view to improve our live-monitoring capabilities:

- **Camera Panels** - Live-monitoring in Overwatch is now just a click away from actual video - when priority events occur, investigate exactly what happened before taking action.

---

#### Platform Performance

We tackled several stability and accuracy improvements in our core tracking, detection, and training pipelines:

- **Track Stability** - Major rework to our tracker improves unstable detection boxes, fragmented tracks, and track switching issues in complex & high-traffic environments.
- **Job Progress** - Improved system visibility with updated process tracking to accurately reflect true job progress states.
- **Imported Models** - Resolved an issue with class-ordering affecting imported models, improving the scalability and portability of Worlds vision models.
